FAST vs. SLOW

The surface of the Earth is constantly changing. Any changes take time to complete. Some of these changes occur very quickly while others take place slowly over thousands of years. The slow changes are the result of weathering, erosion, and deposition. Below are some examples of both fast and slow changes to the Earth's surface.
