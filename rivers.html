Lab Instructions
  • Pack soil into two beakers. Turn each beaker upside down in one end of an aluminum pan.
  • Pat the soil with your hands into a thick, flat layer. The soil should cover 1/4 of one end of the pan.
  • Fill a small spray bottle with water. Adjust the nozzle so that it sprays a hard stream of water. Hold the spray bottle about 5 cm away from the top edge of the soil.
  • Spray water from the bottle until you run out of water. Spray in the same place every time. Do not make a new water path.
  • Observe the area where the water hit the soil to see a small canyon.
  • Observe the area that is underwater to see a small river delta.
